About Me

Md. Mahedi Hassan is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Daffodil International University, where he has been a full-time faculty member since January 1st, 2022. Previously, he worked as an adjunct lecturer at BRAC University in the same department from January 29th, 2021, to August 31st, 2023.

He is an engineer with a strong background in competitive programming, which influences his passion for teaching courses like Programming and Problem Solving, Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures, and Algorithms. Md. Mahedi Hassan is currently preparing to apply for a PhD in Deep Learning or Artificial Intelligence for Spring/Fall 2026. He is originally from Comilla and currently resides in Dhaka.

He graduated from BRAC University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (2017-2021) and achieved a CGPA of 3.97/4.00, graduating with "Highest Distinction". He was also a recipient of the Vice Chancellor's Honor List, Dean's Honor List, and merit-based scholarships. He was also the Champion of the Intra-University Programming Contest (2018).
